## 7. SSC/TK Readiness Assessment

### Strengths
- **Strong core.** Top 5 DPS are all parsing 85%+ and improving. Devgru, Voren, and Dotnhottie are elite for their gear level.
- **Excellent attendance.** 17 players at 100%, 22 at 83%+. Raid leader doesn't need to bench-manage.
- **Farm is clean.** Zero wipes on Gruul in 6 weeks. Mag clean since week 3. Content is trivially on farm.
- **Classic veterans.** This guild cleared through Naxx in Classic. The raid awareness and commitment are there.
- **Improving.** 14/17 DPS trending upward. The guild is getting better every week.

### Concerns
- **Roster depth.** Running 22 instead of 25. SSC/TK requires consistent 25-man rosters with specific class needs (multiple warlocks for Seed of Corruption on Hydross/Lurker adds, etc.).
- **Bottom-tier DPS.** Grievous, Notguldan, Graime, and Hopped are leaving ~1,200-1,500 combined DPS on the table. On tight SSC/TK enrages, this matters.
- **Notguldan is not improving.** 8 weeks of flat performance in a raid where everyone else is getting better is a coaching problem, not a gear problem.
- **Graime's gear.** iLvl 100 will not be viable for SSC/TK. This needs to be resolved before progression.
- **Netherspite variance in Kara** (81s to 343s kill time) suggests inconsistent execution on portal-style mechanics. SSC/TK has similar coordination checks (Hydross transitions, Lurker spout, Alar platform management).

### Recommendations for SSC/TK Prep
1. **Recruit 3-5 players** — especially another Warlock who can pump.
2. **Direct intervention on Notguldan** — sit down, review logs together, fix the Incinerate problem. This player has hit a plateau that won't resolve on its own.
3. **Gear funnel Graime** — or accept that the slot would be better filled by an alt or recruit.
4. **Enforce consumable standards** — Flask + Flame Cap + Food + Drums. No exceptions on progression.
5. **Practice CD management now** — double CDs on Gruul farm is free practice for tighter SSC/TK timers.
6. **Fix the Sunder/Expose Armor confusion** — clarify one person owns the armor debuff, everyone else DPSes.
